Privacy Policy

Access

For any type of access, we have the right to (and also the right to not to) collect the following personal information:

  • Time of access, and
  • IP address.

Note that there might be additional information to collect; refer to their privacy policy.

Web accesses

For web accesses, Toyshost Services will collect the following personal information:

  • Time of site access,
  • IP address, and
  • User Agent.

We collect these information to:

  • detecting and preventing Denial of Service attacks, and
  • to debug out service.

Some other information, if any, may be collected by different services. The full list of information collected and the purpose of collection will be provided by the services.

Web Analytics Tracker

We are aware about your privacy, so we don't use third-party services like Google Analytic. Instead, we use a self-hosted Analyzer, hosted on owa.thost3.de. It will collect:

  • Anything that we can collect from web entries,
  • Webpage rendering result, and
  • Request body.

All IPs are anonymized, by removing the last octet.

You can simply block all connections to owa.thost3.de to stop the tracker.

Cloudflare

All traffics going to thost3.de are routed via Cloudflare. By using any Toyshost Services hosted on thost3.de, you agree to the Cloudflare Privacy Policy as well as ours.
